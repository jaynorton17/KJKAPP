The legacy prompt for the “Most Likely To” (also known as “Who’s More Likely To”) game asked the model to generate full CSV rows with questions starting “Who is most likely to…”, to leave options blank because the app supplies Jay, Kim, Both and Neither, and to use a single question type (`Multiple Choice`).  It listed categories such as Funny, Household, Romance, Chaos, Social Life, Money, Jealousy, Confidence, Spicy and Future, and encouraged variation in tone, intensity and subject matter.  That monolithic prompt has been superseded by this structured JSON approach.
