The previous prompt for the “True or False” game instructed the model to generate CSV rows containing declarative statements about Jay, Kim or both, without including the words “true” or “false”.  It listed categories such as trust, communication, jealousy, habits, romance, conflict, confidence, future, spicy and playful, and emphasised that the app supplies the answer buttons.  It asked for variation in tone and intensity and warned against obvious statements.  That monolithic prompt has been replaced by this structured JSON specification.
